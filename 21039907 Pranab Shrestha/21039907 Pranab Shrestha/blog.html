Effects of Technology

My smartphone, out of all of my gadgets, definitely provides me with the greatest benefits. I'm grateful that its map applications can direct me and my car to my location and propose alternate routes if traffic is heavy. It plays any music I choose and pulls up my e-mails so I can work even while I'm stopped at a red light. I can tour cities all around the world in seconds thanks to the applications, websites, and programs. It sends me amusing internet gifs and memes, and I've even made real professional connections through its social media channels. I admire and genuinely admire you at times.

I even learned many things online which helped me in the assignments and learned new hobbies. During the lockdown, I had many courses of entertainment which was based on technology such as games which I played a lot and made new friends through in-game voice chats which were not available before which was later upgraded in the game.


Technology has its upsides and downsides as everything does everyone does spend more time on their phones and laptops scrolling through social media, playing games, watching movies, and all.
